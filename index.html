Frequently Asked

Common questions.

Is White Sand Funding a lender?

No. White Sand Funding is not a lender. We are an independent funding & credit strategist. All funding you receive comes from third-party lenders (issuing banks), is subject to their approval, and takes the form of business credit cards or business lines of credit. We help you prepare and present your profile so you get the strongest possible approvals.

Will pre-qualification hurt my credit?

No. Pre-qualification involves no hard credit pull — only your self-reported score range. Hard inquiries only occur when we sequence your actual card applications with your written authorization, and we design the sequencing to minimize their impact.

How much funding can I expect?

It depends on your credit profile, income, existing accounts, and which strategy fits you. Stronger profiles tend to access higher 0% business credit limits. Lower scores require credit optimization first. Your strategy call will give you a realistic, no-hype estimate based on your specific situation.

What credit score do I need?

A 700+ personal FICO is typically required to access the strongest 0% offers immediately. If you're below 700, we can still help — we'll start with credit optimization and tradelines to get you into approval range first.

What if my business is brand new?

That's fine. New entities qualify for many no-doc business cards based on the owner's personal credit. For clients who want immediate aged-entity credibility, we offer shelf corporations with EIN history and clean ownership transfer.

How long does the whole process take?

Most clients see their first funding round within 14–21 days if their profile is approval-ready. If credit work is needed first, expect 60–120 days before the first funding round.

What happens after the 0% intro period ends?

That's a core part of what we teach. Strategies include: paying down before the intro ends, recycling into new 0% offers as your profile improves, and rolling balances into longer-term lines of credit. We coach you through the next 12–18 months — we don't disappear after funding.
